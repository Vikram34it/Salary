Compliance Checklist (New Labour Code 2026)

Key Notes - New Labour Code on Social Security 2020 (Effective 2026)

  • EPF Scheme 2026 replaces EPF Scheme 1952 w.e.f. 29 June 2026 under Code on Social Security, 2020
  • EPF contribution rates unchanged: Employee 12%, Employer 12% (8.33% EPS + 3.67% EPF) on basic+DA up to ₹15,000/month ceiling
  • ESIC: Employee 0.75%, Employer 3.25% for gross wages up to ₹21,000/month
  • Professional Tax varies by state - Karnataka exempts up to ₹25,000/month (as of Apr 2025)
  • Minimum wages revised half-yearly (April & October) based on CPI-IW
  • Universal Account Number (UAN) remains the primary PF account identifier
  • Digital processes and electronic filing now formally recognized under the new scheme
  • Principal employer liable when labour contractor defaults on PF contributions
